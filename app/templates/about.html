Game Modes

📉
Market Simulation
Trade AAPL, MSFT, NVDA, AMZN, GOOGL, META, and TSLA on a live order book anchored to real market prices. Place limit orders, manage your position, and track unrealised P&L as prices move. The order book matches buyers and sellers using strict price-time priority — exactly how stock exchanges work.
🃏
5Os — Quartile Estimation
A trading-floor classic. Players estimate the five key statistics (minimum, 25th percentile, median, 75th percentile, and maximum) of a hidden dataset. The market resolves against the true values — perfect calibration wins.
📰
Headline Trading
News hits the wire and prices start moving. React to market-moving headlines, take directional positions, and close out before time runs out. Learn how fast-money traders parse information and act.
🃏
Poker Auction
Bid for poker cards in a sealed-bid auction, assemble the best hand you can, and trade cards with other players in a post-auction market. Combines probability, valuation, and competitive strategy.
🧠
Mental Math
Speed and accuracy in arithmetic is a prerequisite for trading floors. Timed questions drill multiplication, division, percentages, and estimation — with a live leaderboard to keep the pressure on.
